# Proposition to host SEAPATH under the LF energy Europe
- It impacts trademarks, contract, governance, and brings it under the IP protection of the EU.
- This will facilitate to have European funds and speed up adoption among European utilities.
- Some projects have already made the move (Powsybl) https://lfenergy.org/powsybl-a-community-led-open-source-project-for-european-grid-sovereignty/
- The branding will stay the same (LF Energy), it is only a question to host the project under the LF Europe.
- Next TSC will have John Mertic from LFEnergy to answer questions.
